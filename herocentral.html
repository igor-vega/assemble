America Chavez

America Chavez believed she was born in the Utopian Parallel, a reality that is out of time and in the presence of the being known as the Demiurge, whose presence she credited with imbuing her with superpowers. In her memory, when Chavez was approximately six years old, the Utopian Parallel was threatened by destruction. Chavez's mothers sacrificed themselves to seal the black holes, resulting in their particles being scattered across the Multiverse itself. Wanting to prove herself as a hero and knowing Utopia didn't require salvation, Chavez ran away from her home and her responsibilities.She traveled across different realities, eventually adopted the moniker of Miss America, and began covertly acting as a superhero. She has been a part of the Teen Brigade and the Young Avengers.

Miss Marvel

Kamala Khan (A.K.A. Ms. Marvel) is a young girl from Jersey City, daughter to Pakistani immigrants, and an inhuman with healing, shapeshift and polymorph powers. She has ben a part of various teams, but the most important ones were the Avengers and the Champions.

Spider-Man

The friendly neighboorhood Spider-man, secretly Peter Parker, is one of Marvel's most famous and prestigious super heroes, residing in New York City, he lives a double life, between a hero that has to save thousands every year, from supervillans, criminals and natural disasters, and a photojournalist, scientist, student and sometimes even corporate CEO, in some interation. He has been a part of teams like the Avengers and The Fantastic Four.

Doctor Strange

Doctor Stephen Strange (Doctor Strange for short) is Earth's Supreme Sorcer, tasked with the duty of protecting it from evil forces and also the keeping the time stone. He is also one of the brightest minds in the Super Hero community, using much oif his thrist from knowledge to improve upon himself and the planet.

Iron Man

One of the most beloved heroes of Earth, and also one of the brightest and most charismatics, Tony Stark has shown constant dedication and improvment in his path to make earth safer, stronger and more prosperous, from upgrading his shinning armors and fighting skills, to developing new and renewable sources of energy. He has been a part of douzens of teams, but his most famous one is obviously The Avengers.

Professor Xavier

The leader of the X-man and a pillar figure for the mutant community, professor Charles Xvier or Professor X is a mutant with telephaty powers, widely considered one of the strongest mutants in terms of powers and one of the smartest, he has been both a source of good and evil for his people.
